# Product Vision

CareerOS is a job-search operating system for serious job seekers.

It is not a generic resume generator and not a bot that blindly applies to hundreds of vacancies. CareerOS exists to help a candidate make better career decisions with evidence.

## Problem

Job seekers usually face five painful problems:

1. Too many vacancies and no clear priority.
2. Weak or generic applications.
3. Resume versions that do not match specific roles.
4. No structured pipeline from vacancy to offer.
5. Poor interview and salary negotiation preparation.

The result is emotional fatigue, low response rates, and underpriced career moves.

## Product promise

CareerOS turns a vacancy into an action plan.

For every opportunity, it should answer:

- Is this role worth applying to?
- Which facts from my resume support this fit?
- What is missing or weak?
- What should I write in the application?
- How should I adapt my resume?
- What interview questions are likely?
- How should I negotiate compensation?

## Target users

Initial target users:

- QA engineers looking for better roles;
- software engineers switching domains;
- fintech and infrastructure professionals;
- candidates applying internationally;
- job seekers who want fewer but better applications.

## Differentiation

Most job products optimize for vacancy discovery.

CareerOS optimizes for decision quality.

The core differentiator is evidence-based career reasoning: every score, recommendation, and generated text should be traceable to candidate evidence and vacancy requirements.

## North Star metric

The long-term north star is not the number of generated applications.

The north star is:

> Qualified interview rate per high-quality application.

Secondary metrics:

- response rate;
- interview conversion rate;
- offer conversion rate;
- salary uplift;
- time saved per application;
- user confidence before interview.

## Product boundaries

CareerOS should not:

- ask for raw job-board passwords;
- mass-apply without user review;
- fabricate experience;
- encourage dishonest resume inflation;
- hide uncertainty in scoring;
- store sensitive career data without clear purpose.

## Long-term vision

CareerOS can become a personal career intelligence layer:

- job-market radar;
- resume and portfolio optimizer;
- interview simulator;
- offer negotiation assistant;
- career transition planner;
- professional reputation dashboard.

The first wedge is simple: analyze one resume against one vacancy and produce an actionable, evidence-backed application plan.
